Clustering

Clustering: Groups similar consumption patterns together based on weather and time features.

K-Means: Partitions data into K clusters where each observation belongs to the cluster with the nearest mean.

DBSCAN: Identifies dense regions separated by sparse regions, detecting outliers.

Hierarchical: Builds a hierarchy of clusters using a dendrogram.

Forecasting

Forecasting: Predicts future electricity demand based on historical patterns and weather.

Ensemble: Combines multiple models for improved accuracy.

Linear Regression: Simple linear model based on feature relationships.

Random Forest: Ensemble of decision trees.

XGBoost: Gradient boosted decision trees that excel at structured data.

Performance Metrics

Loading performance metrics...
Understanding Metrics:
  • MAE (Mean Absolute Error): Average magnitude of errors (lower is better)
  • RMSE (Root Mean Square Error): Square root of the average squared errors (lower is better)
  • MAPE (Mean Absolute Percentage Error): Average percentage error (lower is better)
  • R²: Proportion of variance explained by the model (higher is better, max 1.0)
  • Improvement: Percentage improvement over the baseline model
